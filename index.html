About

NiteLyfe is the best way to find things to do. The NiteLyfe app shows you what is going on at the businesses around you.

  • Drink Specials
  • Events
  • Concerts
  • Festivals
  • And More!

With the NiteLyfe app, you can easily find where your Facebook friends are going! With NiteLyfe, finding things to do around you has never been easier.

How does NiteLyfe work?

By downloading the NiteLyfe app and connecting to your existing Facebook account, NiteLyfe cross references the events that local businesses post with your friends list! This then lets you know what friends have shown interest in going to different events!

In addition to this, you can look on NiteLyfe's map to find events around you!

  • Does the bar down the street have a drink special?
  • Is the movie theater having a special showing?
  • How about the theater? Is your favorite preformer in town?

NiteLyfe allows for these local businesses to show the people around town exactly whats happening any given night!
